Quick Facts:

  • Produced by: Walt Disney Pictures
  • Year released: 2021
  • Rating: PG (brief rude humor and some action)
  • Original language: English
  • Type of film: animated (3D), coming of age, fantasy

Story

Luca, a sea monster who can disguise as a human on land, decides to adventure beyond the ocean depths with his friend Alberto.

My Thoughts

This movie is extremely charming and will pull at your heartstrings. From the cute animation style, the Italian music, the themes about acceptance and living life as a marginalized person, and the quirky humor, all of it is great. It's a simple film and it works.
