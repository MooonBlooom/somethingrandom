Quick Facts:

Story

Rookie hunter Robyn moves to a town in Ireland where wolves are thought to be from the devil. Out in the forest one day, Robyn meets Mebh, a Wolfwalker, and she becomes what her father and the townsfolk fear most.

My Thoughts

This is my FAVORITE movie! Everything about it is just perfect. The message of questioning authority and embracing your true identity, the gorgeous hand-drawn animation, the vibrant colors, the character designs, the voice acting, the inclusion of "Running with the Wolves" by AURORA, just *chef's kiss*! Cartoon Saloon needs to be supported as it's clear how much love is put into this masterpiece.
