Story

Dipper and Mabel Pines are spending the summer with their Grunkle Stan at his tourist trap The Mystery Shack in Gravity Falls. But not everything is as it seems in this town, especially when Dipper finds a mysterious Journal in the woods.

My Thoughts

This show is loved for a reason. The memes, the mysteries, the lovable characters, and just the weirdness of it all make it a classic, even if I prefer The Owl House.
