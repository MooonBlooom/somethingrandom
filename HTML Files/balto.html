Story

When diptheria breaks out in the small town of Nome, Alaska, the wolf-dog Balto decides to help despite the rejection he's faced from humans and dogs alike.

My Thoughts

There are so many things about this movie that are just amazing. First of all, the animation is gorgeous. The character designs are all unique but not overly complex. James Horner's score perfectly captures the tone of every scene. Most importantly, as someone who has many parts of my identity that mainstream society does not understand (or want to), the message of loving and embracing your inner self is so important.
