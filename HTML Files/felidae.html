Quick Facts:

  • Produced by: TFC Trickompany
  • Year released: 1994
  • Rating: R (language, lots of gore, animal experimentation, and some sexual content)
  • Original language: German
  • Type of film: animated (2D), mystery, thriller

Story

Housecat Francis has just moved to a new neighborhood with his owner Gus and barely has time to get settled in before he is thrust into a feline murder mystery.

My Thoughts

Felidae is definitely not a film for everyone. But for fans of dark, gritty stories and atypical animation, it's a real gem! It's aware of the fact that most animated films are seen as "for kids" and it takes advantage of it. It's gorey and very adult, but rarely comes off as trying too hard. If only it had more screen time to flesh itself out more. If it sounds like your thing, I HIGHLY recommend it!

For my fellow English speakers, I recommend the German audio with English subtitles. The English dub is okay, but it makes Francis sound plain and misses some important details in the ending.
