Quick Facts:

  • Produced by: Walt Disney Productions
  • Year released: 2022
  • Rating: PG (crude humor, action)
  • Original language: English
  • Type of film: animated (3D), coming of age, comedy

Story

Meilin Lee's life was going so smoothly until one night she has a nightmare and wakes up with the ability to turn into a giant, fluffy red panda when she has strong emotions.

My Thoughts

This film has the privilege of being the movie that's made me cry the hardest. The story hits way too close to home. Besides that, the art style is unique, the original songs by FINNEAS and Billie Eilish are bops, and the complicated relationship between Mei, her mom, and her grandmother is engaging and realistic.
