Quick Facts:

  • Produced by: Sony Pictures Animation

  • Original language: English
  • Type of film: animated (3D), mockumentary

Story

Young rockhopper penguin Cody Maverick travels from his home in Shiverpool, Antarctica to the famous Pen Gu Island to participate in the 10th Annual Big Z Memorial Surf-off.

My Thoughts

One watch-through of this film and a peek at the behind-the-scenes material is all it takes to see just how much love was put into it. The effort the animators took to make the waves scientifically accurate, the improvisation, the loyalty to surf culture, the custom-made camera they made to make it feel like an actual documentary. Add that to lovable characters, animation that still looks gorgerous today, and a much-needed message about humility and appreciating things for their own sake and you get a masterpiece.
